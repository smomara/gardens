Welcome to My Garden

This is a digital garden - a space for thoughts to grow and connect naturally, rather than disappear down a itmeline. It's built using Garden, a static site generator I built to emphasize discovery through exploration rather than chronology.

Here you'll find my reflections on programming and technology, musings on faith and virtue, and explorations of whatever else captures my curiosity.

I believe the best ideas grow slowly and connect unexpectedly. That's why I built this space to let you explore at your own pace. You can learn more about me and why I write, or just start wandering through the garden of ideas.

The backlinks on each page will show you related thoughts, letting you follow whatever path interest you most.

If something catches your attention or you'd like to discuss further, feel free to get in touch. I'm always eager to explore new ideas.
